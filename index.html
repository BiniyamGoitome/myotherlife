Privacy Policy for My Other Life

Effective Date: April 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to My Other Life ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide:

  • Account information (when you connect to Google Play Games)
  • Game progress and save data
  • Character collection data
  • Achievement data

2.2 Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device information (model, OS version, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • In-app purchase history

2.3 Third-Party Services We Use:

Google Play Services:

  • Collects: Device information, app usage, crash reports
  • Purpose: Leaderboards, achievements, game saves
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob:

  • Collects: Device information, advertising ID, app usage
  • Purpose: Display advertisements
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase Analytics:

  • Collects: App usage, device information, user actions
  • Purpose: App improvement and crash reporting
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google In-App Purchases:

  • Collects: Purchase history
  • Purpose: Process gem purchases and remove ads
  • Note: We do not store your payment information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Save your game progress
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Improve App performance
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Respond to user support requests

4. Data Storage

  • Game progress is stored locally on your device
  • Optional cloud save uses Google Play Games Services
  • Purchase data is handled by Google Play

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection for advertising

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App.
